La Cocina Menu

Enjoy our selection of authentic Mexican dishes. Our food is prepared by an experienced chef and based on family recipes. All meats mentioned in the menu can be replaced with your selection of ground beef, steak, shredded chicken, grilled chicken, or shredded pork. Any toppings or sides may be ommitted upon request. All orders include complementary chips and salsa.
